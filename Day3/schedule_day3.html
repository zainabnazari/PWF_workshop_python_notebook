Dates: 29 Sept – 1 Oct 2025   |   Format: Online via Google Meet

Time Zone: Central European Time (CET/CEST), Rome

Workshop Overview

This 3-day workshop introduces machine learning foundations and their applications in physics. The first two days cover core programming and machine learning methods, while Day 3 is dedicated to applications in physics, featuring international experts.

Day 1 & 2 – Foundations

Day 1: Python programming, data analysis, and visualization. Day 2: Core ML concepts – classification, regression, clustering, and neural networks.

Day 3 – Wednesday, 1 October 2025

Focus: Applications of Machine Learning in Physics   |   Chair: Kate Shaw
